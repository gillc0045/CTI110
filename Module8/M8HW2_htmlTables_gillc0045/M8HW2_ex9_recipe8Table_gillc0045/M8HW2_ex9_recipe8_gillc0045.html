Chili Bean Recipe
Ingredients Seasoning
1 pound of hamburger meat pepper
bag of kidney beans sea salt
tomatoes cayenne pepper
onions tomato sauce
Cooking directions:
  • boil water with sea salt
  • add beans while boiling
  • turn the temperature down to low after a few minutes
  • add cayenne pepper to beans
  • cook hamburger meat and season with pepper
  • when the beans are almost soft add meat, tomato sauce and onions
